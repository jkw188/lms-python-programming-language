Recursion

The Russian Nesting Doll Analogy

Function Call fact(5)
fact(4)
BASE CASE

Recursion is like opening a set of dolls.

1. You open the biggest doll (the function call) to find a smaller one inside.
2. You keep opening them until you reach the tiniest, solid doll (the Base Case).
3. Once you reach the center, you put them all back together (returning values up the chain).
